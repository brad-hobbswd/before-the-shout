About the Author

Bradley Hobbs is a teacher, writer, and devoted follower of Jesus Christ who believes that lasting faith is built long before it is tested. His passion is to encourage ordinary believers to develop a quiet, steadfast walk with God that remains strong through every season of life.

Drawing from Scripture, personal reflection, and years of serving within the local church, Bradley writes with the conviction that spiritual maturity is formed through daily obedience, consistent prayer, and an unwavering trust in the unchanging character of Christ. His desire is not merely to inspire emotional moments but to point readers toward a foundation that will endure when life becomes uncertain.

As an educator, he values thoughtful discipleship and believes that the greatest lessons are often learned in ordinary moments. As an author, his hope is that every page leads readers beyond temporary excitement and into a deeper relationship with Jesus, the only sure foundation.

If this book has encouraged you, challenged you, or strengthened your faith, may the glory belong to God alone. May you continue to seek His presence, treasure His Word, and discover that the Rock beneath your feet is faithful in every season.

Grace and peace be with you as you continue building your life upon Jesus Christ, the only sure foundation.

May your worship remain joyful, your prayer life remain steadfast, your faith continue to grow, and your hope remain anchored in the One who never changes.

May you discover every day that the Rock beneath your feet is faithful through every season of life.

Jesus was always enough.

Thank you for taking this journey. May the Lord establish every step you take upon His unshakable foundation.
