Dedication

This book is dedicated to every believer who has ever wondered if they were strong enough to endure the storm.

To those who have worshipped with tears in their eyes, prayed through uncertainty, and continued walking by faith when emotions could no longer carry them.

To those who have discovered that a quiet life of obedience is just as beautiful as a joyful shout of praise.

May these pages encourage you to build every thought, every decision, every relationship, and every hope upon Jesus Christ, the sure and unshakable foundation.

With gratitude to my family, my church family, and every person who has encouraged me to keep writing, keep praying, and keep trusting the Lord.

Above all, this work is dedicated to Jesus Christ, whose mercy is new every morning and whose faithfulness remains when every other foundation fails.
